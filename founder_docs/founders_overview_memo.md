# PropFolio — Founders Overview Memo

**Audience:** Founders  
**Purpose:** Single-page overview of the founder document package and how it supports operations, fundraising, IP, and launch.  
**Date:** 2025-03-12

---

## What This Package Is

A **founder document package** for a U.S. startup (PropFolio) launching an iOS consumer app. It includes:

- **Governance & roles** — who does what, how decisions are made, how deadlocks are broken
- **Equity & vesting** — split framework, vesting schedule, cap table template
- **IP & confidentiality** — draft IP assignment, PIIA/CIIA, contractor and advisor frameworks
- **Corporate hygiene** — corporate records, brand/trademark tracking, data room checklist
- **Fundraising** — investor readiness, narrative outline
- **Launch & risk** — product risk disclosures, privacy/data governance, App Store launch governance

---

## Why It Matters

- **Investors** expect clear cap tables, vesting, IP assignment, and corporate records.
- **Counsel** can turn these drafts and checklists into signed agreements and policies.
- **Launch** (App Store, privacy, terms) requires documented governance and compliance steps.
- **Founders** reduce ambiguity on roles, equity, and decision-making up front.

## Attorney Review

The following should be reviewed by qualified U.S. counsel before adoption or signing:

- **IP assignment** and **confidentiality/invention assignment** drafts
- **Equity split** and **vesting** when converted into stock/option agreements or plan documents
- **Decision-making and deadlock** when reflected in bylaws, operating agreement, or shareholder agreements
- **Advisor equity** when drafting formal advisor agreements

Checklists and memos are operational guides only; they are not legal advice.

---

## PropFolio Context

- **Product:** iOS-first real estate investment intelligence app; primary question: "Should I buy this property?"
- **Business model:** Subscription (Pro) with free tier (limited imports).
- **Launch:** App Store submission; privacy policy, terms, support URL, subscription and account deletion compliant.
- **Risks to disclose:** App is informational only; not investment, tax, or legal advice; users must verify data.

Use the package to align founder agreements, corporate records, and launch governance with this context.
