# PropFolio — Corporate Records Checklist

**Purpose:** Maintain corporate hygiene for a U.S. startup; supports fundraising and due diligence.  
**Audience:** Founders, operations  
**Date:** 2025-03-12

---

## 1. Formation and Charter

| # | Item | Location / action | Done |
|---|------|-------------------|------|
| 1 | Certificate of Incorporation (or LLC equivalent) filed | Secretary of State; keep certified copy | ☐ |
| 2 | Bylaws (or Operating Agreement) adopted and signed | Corporate records | ☐ |
| 3 | Initial director(s) and officer(s) designated | Consent or minutes | ☐ |
| 4 | EIN obtained (IRS) | IRS; keep confirmation | ☐ |
| 5 | Registered agent and address current | State filing | ☐ |

---

## 2. Equity and Cap Table

| # | Item | Location / action | Done |
|---|------|-------------------|------|
| 6 | Cap table maintained and updated | [cap_table_template.csv](cap_table_template.csv) or equivalent | ☐ |
| 7 | Stock purchase agreements (founders) signed and filed | Secure folder | ☐ |
| 8 | 83(b) elections (if any) filed with IRS and Company | Copy in records | ☐ |
| 9 | Option plan (if any) approved by board; grant forms signed | Plan doc + individual grants | ☐ |
| 10 | Advisor agreements and option/RS grants documented | [advisor_equity_framework.md](advisor_equity_framework.md); cap table | ☐ |

## 5. Contracts and Compliance

| # | Item | Location / action | Done |
|---|------|-------------------|------|
| 18 | Material contracts (partnerships, SaaS, revenue share) signed and filed | Contract log + secure storage | ☐ |
| 19 | Insurance (e.g., D&O, E&O, cyber) in place if applicable | Policy documents; renewal dates | ☐ |
| 20 | State and local filings (e.g., annual report, franchise tax) current | Calendar reminders | ☐ |

---

## 6. Data Room Readiness

- Keep a **clean copy** of key records (charter, bylaws, cap table, key contracts, IP assignments) for investor due diligence.
- See [due_diligence_data_room_checklist.md](due_diligence_data_room_checklist.md) for structure.

---

## 7. Review Cadence

- **Quarterly:** Cap table, option grants, material contracts.
- **Annually:** Bylaws/charter alignment, state filings, insurance, trademark renewals.
- **Before fundraising:** Full pass using [investor_readiness_checklist.md](investor_readiness_checklist.md).
