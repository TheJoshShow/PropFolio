# PropFolio Founder Documents

This folder contains the **founder document package** for PropFolio, a U.S. startup launching an iOS consumer app (real estate investment intelligence). The package supports:

- **Startup operations** — roles, decision-making, corporate records
- **Fundraising readiness** — cap table, investor checklist, narrative, data room
- **IP protection** — IP assignment, confidentiality, contractor checklist, brand/trademark
- **Launch governance** — App Store, privacy, product risk disclosures

## Attorney Review

Documents that are **drafts** or contain **legal commitments** should be reviewed by qualified U.S. counsel before use:

- **ip_assignment_agreement_draft.md**
- **confidentiality_and_invention_assignment_draft.md**
- **founder_equity_split_framework.md** (when converting to actual agreements)
- **vesting_schedule_framework.md** (when drafting option plans or stock agreements)
- **founder_decision_making_and_deadlock_policy.md** (when reflected in bylaws or operating agreements)
- **advisor_equity_framework.md** (when drafting advisor agreements)

Checklists, templates, and memos are operational guides; they do not replace legal advice. Where a document says **"[Attorney review recommended]"** or **"Draft — not legal advice"**, have an attorney review before signing or filing.

## How to Use

1. **Read** [founders_overview_memo.md](founders_overview_memo.md) first.
2. **Customize** each document for your entity name, state of formation, and founder names.
3. **Complete** checklists and CSVs as you operate (corporate records, cap table, brand, data room).
4. **Route** drafts to counsel for review before signing or adopting as binding policy.
5. **Update** documents when roles, cap table, or governance change.
