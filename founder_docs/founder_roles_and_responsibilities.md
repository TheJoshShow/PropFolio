# PropFolio — Founder Roles and Responsibilities

**Purpose:** Define roles, titles, and responsibility allocation among founders to reduce ambiguity and support governance.  
**Audience:** Founders, future board/investors  
**Date:** 2025-03-12

---

## 1. Principles

- Roles should be **documented** and **agreed** in writing (e.g., in a founder agreement or board resolutions).
- Responsibilities can evolve; this document is a **baseline** to update as the company grows.
- **Decision authority** (e.g., day-to-day vs. material decisions) should align with [founder_decision_making_and_deadlock_policy.md](founder_decision_making_and_deadlock_policy.md).

---

## 2. Role Definitions (Template)

Customize for your team. Example structure for a small founding team:

| Role | Title (example) | Primary responsibilities | Decision authority (example) |
|------|------------------|---------------------------|-----------------------------|
| **Founder 1** | CEO / Co-Founder | Strategy, fundraising, partnerships, external communications, final say on material contracts and hiring above threshold | Day-to-day operations; material decisions per deadlock policy |
| **Founder 2** | CTO / Co-Founder | Product, engineering, technical architecture, security and infrastructure, release process | Day-to-day technical and product decisions; material decisions per deadlock policy |
| **Founder 3** (if any) | COO / Co-Founder or other | Operations, finance, legal coordination, corporate records, compliance | Day-to-day operations in scope; material decisions per deadlock policy |

## 4. Titles and Public Representation

- **Public titles** (website, App Store, LinkedIn) should match internal roles and not overstate authority.
- **Spokesperson** for press and investors: typically CEO unless otherwise designated.
- **Technical / product** representation: typically CTO.

---

## 5. Changes to Roles

- Role or responsibility changes should be **agreed** by all founders (or per decision policy).
- **Document** changes in writing (board consent, founder agreement amendment, or memo to file).
- Update this document and any related governance docs when roles change.
