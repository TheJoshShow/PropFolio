# PropFolio — Advisor Equity Framework

**Purpose:** Principles and practices for compensating advisors with equity; supports consistent and defensible advisor grants.  
**Audience:** Founders; counsel when drafting advisor agreements  
**Date:** 2025-03-12

---

## 1. Disclaimer

**[Attorney review recommended]** when granting advisor equity. Securities law (e.g., registration, exemptions), tax (e.g., 83(b), 409A), and company cap table implications require counsel review. This document is a **framework** only.

---

## 2. When to Use Advisor Equity

- **Advisors** provide guidance, introductions, or domain expertise but are **not** employees or contractors doing core product/engineering work.
- **Formalize** in a written **advisor agreement** that covers: role, term, time commitment, equity grant (or promise), vesting, and confidentiality.

---

## 3. Typical Grant Sizes (Market Reference)

*These are illustrative ranges; market and stage vary.*

| Stage / role | Typical range (fully diluted) | Vesting |
|--------------|-------------------------------|---------|
| **Informal / light** | 0.10% – 0.25% | 1–2 years; often monthly, no cliff or 3–6 month cliff |
| **Active advisor (e.g., 5–10 hrs/mo)** | 0.25% – 0.50% | 1–2 years |
| **Heavy / strategic (e.g., formal advisory board)** | 0.50% – 1.0% | 1–2 years; sometimes 2-year cliff |

- **Cap:** Many companies cap total advisor pool (e.g., 2–5% of fully diluted) to preserve option pool for employees.
- **Document** each grant in an advisor agreement and update [cap_table_template.csv](cap_table_template.csv).

---

## 4. Vesting and Form of Grant

- **Vesting:** Typically **1–2 years**, **monthly** vesting; **no cliff** or short cliff (e.g., 3–6 months). Unlike founders, advisors usually do not have a 1-year cliff.
- **Form:** Options (ISO or NSO) or restricted stock (RS). Options are common to preserve 409A and avoid immediate tax; grant at fair market value. **[Attorney and tax advisor review required.]**
- **Start date:** Vesting usually starts on effective date of advisor agreement or first meeting, as specified in the agreement.

---

## 5. Advisor Agreement Contents (Checklist)

- [ ] Name, role, and term (e.g., 12 months; renewable).
- [ ] Time commitment (e.g., up to 5 hours per month; availability for calls/email).
- [ ] Equity: number of shares or options, strike (if options), vesting schedule.
- [ ] Confidentiality and (if applicable) non-disparagement; no IP assignment unless advisor is creating deliverables (then use [contractor_ip_and_confidentiality_checklist.md](contractor_ip_and_confidentiality_checklist.md)).
- [ ] No employment; no guarantee of continued relationship; termination and effect on unvested equity.
- [ ] Acknowledgment that grant is subject to company’s option plan and board approval.
- [ ] Signatures and date.

---

## 6. Tracking

- Maintain a **log** of advisor grants: name, date, amount, vesting, agreement on file.
- Update [cap_table_template.csv](cap_table_template.csv) with a row per advisor (e.g., security_type = “Option” or “RS”, holder_type = “Advisor”).
- Ensure total advisor equity stays within any internal cap and is approved by board (or founders if pre-board).
